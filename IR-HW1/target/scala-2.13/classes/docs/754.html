SENIOR DELEGATES PESSIMISTIC ON ICO TALKS

Efforts to break an impasse between coffee exporting and importing countries over regulating the world coffee market in the face of falling prices appear to have failed, senior delegates said after a contact group meeting. The full ICO council is due to meet this evening. President of the Brazilian Coffee Institute (IBC) Jorio Dauster told s after the contact group meeting there had been no agreement on quotas as consumers had tried to dictate market shares rather than negotiate them. Dauster said there are no plans yet to renew negotiations at a later date.
