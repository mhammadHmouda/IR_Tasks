NOLAND NOLD FEBRUARY SALES OFF TWO PCT

Noland Co said February sales were off 2.3 pct to 29.4 mln dlrs from 300.1 mln dlrs a year earlier, with year-to-date sales off 9.6 pct to 55.3 mln dlrs from 61.2 mln dlrs. The company blamed extremely disruptive winter weather in many of its markets.
