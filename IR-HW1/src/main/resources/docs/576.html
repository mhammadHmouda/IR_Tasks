CINCINNATI BELL CSN SETS STOCK SPLIT

Cincinnati Bell Inc said its board declared a two-for-one stock split, subject to two thirds approval at the annual meeting on April 20 of an increase in authorized common shares to 120 mln from 60 mln. It said the split would be payable May 20 to holders of record May Five.
