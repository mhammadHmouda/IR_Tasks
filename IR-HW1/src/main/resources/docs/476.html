REUTER DEALING SERVICE INTRODUCED IN CHINA

s Holdings Plc RTRS.L said it had its first subscriber installation in China of its foreign exchange dealing service. The subscriber is the banking department of the China International Trust and Investment Corp's (CITIC) head office in Beijing. The service is also due to be connected soon at the Bank of China's new headquarters in Beijing, s said.
