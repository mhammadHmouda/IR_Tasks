MARRIOTT MHS TO SELL HOTEL

Four Seasons Hotels said it and VMS Realty Partners of Chicago have agreed to purchase the Santa Barbara Biltmore Hotel from Marriott Corp for an undisclosed amount. It said the venture will rename the hotel the Four Seasons Biltmore at Santa Barbara and invest over 13 mln dlrs in improvements on the 228-room property.
