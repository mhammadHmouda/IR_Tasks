AMERICAN NURSERY ANSY BUYS FLORIDA NURSERY

American Nursery Products Inc said it purchased Miami-based Heinl's Nursery Inc, for undisclosed terms. Heinl's Nursery has sales of about 4.5 mln dlrs and owns 100 acres, of which 75 are in shade houses and about 58,300 square feet cover greenhouses, shipping and office facilities.
